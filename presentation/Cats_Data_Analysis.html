Anatomical Data from Domestic Cats

Description

The heart and body weights of samples of male and female cats used fordigitalisexperiments. The cats were all adult, over 2 kg body weight.

This data frame contains the following columns:

  • Sex: Factor with evelsFandM
  • Bwt: body weight in kg
  • Hwt: heart weight in g
